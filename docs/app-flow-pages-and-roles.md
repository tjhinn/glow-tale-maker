# app-flow-pages-and-roles.md — YourFairyTale.ai

## 🧭 Site Map (Top-Level Pages)
1. **Homepage** — Begin your story  
2. **Personalization Form** — Meet your hero  
3. **Story Selection** — Choose your adventure  
4. **Preview Page** — See the magic  
5. **Checkout** — Pay securely  
6. **Thank You / Download** — Receive your book  
7. **Admin Dashboard** — Manage stories & orders

---

## 🎯 Purpose of Each Page
| Page | Purpose |
|------|----------|
| Homepage | Inspire and invite parents to start. |
| Personalization Form | Gather child data step by step. |
| Story Selection | Let users choose their fairy-tale theme. |
| Preview Page | Display watermarked sample + sharing reward. |
| Checkout | Secure Stripe payment and discount logic. |
| Thank You | Confirm purchase and deliver PDF link. |
| Admin Dashboard | Maintain catalog, orders, and user data. |

---

## 👥 User Roles & Access
| Role | Permissions |
|------|--------------|
| **Guest User** | Create personalized story, checkout, receive link. |
| **Admin** | Add/edit stories, manage orders, process refunds. |
| **System** | Handle PDF generation, email delivery, link expiry. |

---

## 🚶 Primary User Journeys
### 1. Parent Journey — “Create & Share”
1. Tap **Start Your Fairy Tale** on homepage.  
2. Fill personalization cards and choose story.  
3. Preview → share for 10 % off → checkout → download.

### 2. Gift-Giver Journey — “Buy Instantly”
1. Visit shared link → view sample → checkout directly → gift PDF.  

### 3. Admin Journey — “Manage & Expand”
1. Log in → add new fairy tale template → monitor orders → upload assets.

---

## ❤️ Summary
Each flow stays under **three intuitive steps**, aligning with the **“Don’t make me think”** principle.  
Every path feels **magical, forgiving, and emotionally kind.**
