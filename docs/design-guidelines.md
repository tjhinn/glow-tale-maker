# design-guidelines.md — YourFairyTale.ai

## ✨ Emotional Thesis
**Feels like stepping into a shimmering bedtime story** — bright, kind, and gently animated.  
Inspired by *Kindness in Design* (from `design-tips.md`) where technology disappears behind warmth and care.

---

## 🖋 Typography
| Role | Font | Style |
|------|------|--------|
| **H1** | Playfair Display / DM Serif Display | Whimsical, elegant serif |
| **H2–H3** | Poppins / Nunito Sans | Rounded, friendly sans |
| **Body** | Inter / Rubik | Clean, legible sans |
| **Line Height** | ≥ 1.5× | For comfort and readability |

---

## 🎨 Color System
| Role | Hex | Purpose |
|------|-----|----------|
| Primary | `#FF8B00` | Warm CTA + highlight |
| Accent | `#7A5FFF` | Magical violet tones |
| Secondary | `#FFE97F` | Soft golden light |
| Background | `#FFFDF8` | Paper-white warmth |
| Text | `#0A0A0A` / `#444444` | High contrast |
| Success | `#5BE37D` | Celebration states |

Maintain ≥ 4.5:1 contrast ratio.

---

## 📏 Spacing & Layout
- Base 8 pt grid.  
- Single-column responsive layout.  
- 12–16 px soft shadows for cards.  
- Vertical rhythm: 12–20 pt.  
- Full-width hero on mobile.

---

## 💫 Motion & Interaction
- **Easing:** spring for delight, ease-in-out for calm.  
- **Duration:** 200–280 ms microinteractions; 400 ms transitions.  
- **Examples:**  
  - CTA hover → sparkle trail  
  - Upload success → soft pop  
  - Checkout success → confetti burst  

Follows *Kindness in Design*: motion that celebrates, never startles.

---

## 🗣 Voice & Tone
**Tone:** nurturing, enthusiastic, magical.  

**Examples:**
- “✨ Your story begins here.”  
- “One last sparkle before checkout!”  
- “The adventure awaits — check your inbox!”

---

## ♿ Accessibility
- Honors `prefers-reduced-motion`.  
- Full keyboard navigation and ARIA labels.  
- Clear focus indicators.  
- Semantic headings + landmarks.  
- WCAG AA+ compliance.

---

## 🧩 System Consistency
- Glowing orange CTAs across all screens.  
- Rounded corners and soft shadows repeated.  
- Sparkle motif as subtle motion theme.  
- Same typographic rhythm across admin and user flows.

---

## ✅ Emotional Audit Checklist
- [ ] Does the interface evoke “magical & bright”?  
- [ ] Is motion kind, not distracting?  
- [ ] Do copy and visuals feel supportive and human?

---

## 🧪 Technical QA Checklist
- Typography matches modular scale.  
- Contrast ratios meet AA+.  
- Interactive states visible and tactile.  
- Animation durations 150–300 ms max unless cinematic.
